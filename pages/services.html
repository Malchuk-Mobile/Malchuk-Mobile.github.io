Our Services

Preventative Maintenance (PM's) and Yard Checks

You're a busy person — don't let everyday truck and trailer concerns get in your way. There are a wide variety of problems that can occur with your units during transportation and Malchuk Mobile has the proper equipment and experience to locate, repair and/or replace any issues that happen within your fleet quickly and effectively.

Better yet, with our PM and Yard Check services, we have the experience to properly maintain your fleet on a scheduled basis and therefore limit the amount of issues your fleets experience during their daily routes.

Truck Repairs/Maintenance

Keeping your truck in reliable working condition relies on necessary repairs and scheduled maintenances. We offer a variety of out-of-shop truck repairs and services. Call us today to discuss how we can get you back on your route!

Please note we are a mobile based company and therefore we do not offer SGI Safety Inspections. However, if a Safety Inspection is needed, we will gladly refer you to a trusted SGI certified repair shop.

Trailer Repairs/Maintenance

Scheduled trailer maintenance is a crucial aspect to maintaining a dependable and sturdy rig. We are experienced in maintaining the condition of your trailers and can provide all trailer work that can be completed out-of-shop — from air brakes and electrical to exterior/interior work including complete containers, chassis, dry van bodies, reefer van bodies, flatbeds, step decks, and converter dollies.

In the case that we cannot fix your trailer on site, we promise to take the necessary steps to ensure a safe arrival at a trusted local truck and trailer repair shop.

Lift Gate Servicing and Repairs

Whether your lift gate needs servicing, maintenance, or repair, Malchuk Mobile has you covered. From diagnosing issues to getting your lift gate back in working order, give us a call and we will make sure your liftgate remains in tip-top shape.

No Starts

Welcome to the prairies! Here in Saskatchewan, winters can be rough. Ensure that you are able to get back on the road when Mother Nature freezes your transport with Malchuk Mobile. Give us a call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and our mobile service truck operator will get you back up and running as promptly as possible.

Reefer and Heater Repairs/Maintenance

Some of the most important components of a trailer system are the reefers and heaters. Used to heat and cool your trailers, these essential components need to be kept in prime working condition to ensure your transported goods make it to their final destination. Contact us for any of your reefer or heater repair and maintenance needs.
